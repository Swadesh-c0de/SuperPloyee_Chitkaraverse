# Cortex Systems Showcase Script

This document outlines the workflow for demonstrating the Superloyee (Cortex) platform using the seeded **Cortex Systems** data.

## Presentation Setup
- **Company Name**: Cortex Systems
- **Industry**: Enterprise AI Infrastructure
- **Core Product**: Neural Knowledge Retrieval

---

## Part 1: Onboarding (The Initialization)
1. **Start at `/onboarding`**.
2. **Company Name**: Enter `Cortex Systems`.
3. **Mission One-Liner**: `The ultimate neural layer for enterprise knowledge retrieval.`
4. **Primary Customer**: `Fortune 500 Enterprises`.
5. **Biggest Problem**: `Information fragmentation across Slack, Notion, and Jira.`
6. **Departments**: Add `Engineering`, `Product`, `Customer Success`, `Legal`.
7. **Use Cases**: Select `SOP Automation`, `Customer Intelligence`, `Knowledge Discovery`.
8. **Action**: Click **INITIALIZE_NEURAL_BASE**. Watch the "Neural Synthesis" animation. This step populates the initial company identity.

## Part 2: The Observatory (Dashboard)
1. **Navigate to `/dashboard`**.
2. **Key Metric**: Point out the **Neural Integrity Status (98%)**. Explain that this is calculated from the consistency of the `seed/analysis/ui-data.json`.
3. **Constellation Graph**: Hover over nodes.
   - Point out **Project Phoenix** (the high-priority Q4 project).
   - Show how it connects to **Security Incident Protocol** and **Cortex Core Engine**.
   - Explain that these links are real, derived from `[[WikiLinks]]` in the seeded Markdown files.
4. **Action**: Search for `Phoenix` in the search bar to see the graph filter in real-time.

## Part 3: SOP Autopilot (Living Procedures)
1. **Navigate to `/tools/sop-autopilot`**.
2. **Scenario**: "We have a high-priority expansion (Project Phoenix). How do we handle security?"
3. **Selection**: Click on **Security Incident Protocol** (Engineering).
4. **Integration**: Show the steps. Point out **Source_Integrity: GitHub Sync**. 
5. **Drift Detection**: Select **Engineer Onboarding** (HR).
   - Show the **Integrity_Warning** (Stale steps). 
   - Explain: "The system detected that while our policy says to use `#general`, recent Slack activity shows we now use `#announcements`. The SOP is automatically marked as outdated."

## Part 4: Sync History (The Nerve Center)
1. **Navigate to `/dashboard/signals`**.
2. **Filtering**: Click on the **GitHub** icon in the sidebar. 
   - Show the sync for `repository/cortex-core`. 
   - Point out the impact on **Cortex Core Engine** and **System Architecture**.
3. **Real GitHub Connection**: 
   - Enter `https://github.com/facebook/react` (or any public repo).
   - Click **CONNECT_GITHUB**.
   - Watch the "ANALYZING_REPOSITORY..." loader.
   - Show the live fetch of the latest commit message and SHA.

## Part 5: Customer Intelligence (Neural Sentiment)
1. **Navigate to `/tools/customer-intelligence`**.
2. **Analysis**: Show the **94% Satisfaction Index**.
3. **Objection Vectors**: Point out that **Security Compliance** is the top objection (82%) and is **ACCELERATING**.
4. **Actionable Insights**: Look at the **Product Requirements**. Show that **SSO Integration** is the #1 request with 112 mentions.
5. **Personas**: Show **The Enterprise Security Lead**. Explain how this persona was generated by analyzing the 1,452 interactions in the seed data.

## Part 6: Feed Sources (Ingestion)
1. **Navigate to `/dashboard/feed`**.
2. **Connectors**: Scroll to the bottom.
3. **Action**: Click **NOTION** or **JIRA**.
   - Show the full-screen "Encrypted OAuth Tunnel" animation.
   - Explain: "We aren't just importing files; we are establishing a deep neural link to the enterprise source of truth."

---

## Conclusion
"Superloyee (Cortex) doesn't just store your data; it builds a living, breathing model of your company. Every metric you see—from SOP health to customer sentiment—is a direct result of neural analysis on your raw company knowledge."
